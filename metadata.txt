name=ZTD-AITD Map Generator
qgisMinimumVersion=3.22
description=Generate Zenith Tropospheric Delay (ZTD) maps from GNSS observations using the (Augmented) Iterative Tropospheric Decomposition strategy.
about=Reads GNSS ZTD observations from a CSV/tabular file (lon, lat, height, ZTD), automatically downloads a matching DEM (keyless AWS Terrain Tiles) or uses a local one, and interpolates dense ZTD maps with the ITD and Augmented ITD (AITD) methods. Outputs four rasters: ZTD map, stratified component, turbulent component and DEM. Implements Song, Venuti, Monti-Guarnieri & Manzoni (2025), Environmental Modelling and Software 194, 106669. Developed by Rohollah Naeijian for the Geoinformatics Project course at Politecnico di Milano.
